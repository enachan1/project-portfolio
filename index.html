Projects

Jun&Cathy Grocery POS and Inventory

This is our capstone project fulfilling the requirements for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. It is a Web-Based Point of Sale and Inventory System dedicated to Jun & Cathy Grocery, our project beneficiary.

Jun & Cathy Grocery has been struggling to manage their inventory since they can't monitor the current stock levels of specific items and can't track their monthly and annual sales. They also use manual methods for monitoring the items they sell, which is time-consuming. So, we proposed this system to them and worked on it for almost 3 months.

This was written in HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, Javascript/JQuery, php, and MySQL is used for database.
